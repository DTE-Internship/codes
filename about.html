About Us

D´Headstone Cheers!

D´Headstone Cheers

D’Headstone cheers is the Social impact initiative of D’Headstone Empire which was founded on impact. D’Headstone cheers is service, charity, enlightenment and empowerment oriented. We are focused and determined on making the life of every individual in disadvantaged situations better and fulfilling, by developing a Total man from childhood to Adulthood, following the process of growth and development of a human child, providing every need essential for a hitch free transition into a better person and provide them the hope of a bright future.

We are committed to making the world a better place through series of impacting platforms, impacting communities and educating them on general human wellness, the essence of living a decent life, responsible consumption and building a strong bond within families and communities.

Touching lives being our watch word wasn’t just stumbled upon, but was carefully coined from the burden we feel seeing children, teenagers and youths from different works of life and communities deprived of an opportunity to thrive through the use of their various giftings and live a fulfilling and impactful life in various sects in the economy.

Quality education, healthcare and psychological attention are what a human child needs to grow into a confident teenager and also a youth (Adult). DHC is committed to being a part of the growth and development of every child we are involved with from childhood to adulthood, including their families and communities providing what is essential for each stage of life.

Sustainable empowerment is one of the basic things a youth needs to be settled in life, we are aimed at providing quality education, healthcare and sustainable empowerment to every individual in need of it.

OUR FOCUS

D’Headstone Cheers stands for uniqueness and quality, we do not have a cliché of words, but have derived a different strategy to impacting lives. We are focused on dealing with individuals from their very young age to adulthood, every person in our care receives equal care and attention. Child care, Life guard and youth empowerment & investment are what we stand for at D’Headstone Cheers. These are the three pillars we are built on.
